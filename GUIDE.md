# Miencrap 2 minerat guide (wip)

this guide explains how to beat each part of miencrap 2 minerat (minimal spoilers)

## Overworld
<details>
  <summary>View</summary>

1. Dehydration: After spawning you will immediately find yourself dying of dehydration! 
If you do not find a water source in the first few seconds you will die so be careful

You will also experience dehydration after some time of not drinking water or not dying.
!! Remember to drink water or die once in a while !!

2. Planks: because it's impossible to get planks from trees you should find them in structures instead
3. Crafting table: find the recipe in the recipe book after getting planks. also look for a big floating pink structure
4. Cobblestone: you need to mine stone to get it. not sand. stone 
5. Structures: 
On a big oil platform structure you will find a """plane""" which is a really useful mob for travelling!

In a floating pink structure you will find a block you will 100% need in your playthrough

6. Mobs:

Shrek - a mob that chases you and insta-kills you if you get caught

Fnisser - a mob that makes really loud sounds and calles shrek when it sees you
It spawns in the shrek village structure along with shrek himself
When it sees you it makes really loud noise and causes shrek to spawn in a nearby """shrek home""" 

NOTJUAN - a boss you are really not supposed to fight 
Has around 65k heath points and can spit lava

Gaming chair - a "boss" that you can actually fight!
Has around 64 health points and doesn't have any other remarkable things about it (aside from its size) 

Dolphin - a really fast hostile mob 
!! DON'T run if you meet it! the best way of escaping it is building up

Pigeon - a neutral (???) mob that shoots lasers
you can feed it with cooked seeds (to get cooked seeds you need a microwave block) and it'll become tame
otherwise it will try to kill you
Sometimes drops a microwave when killed

Puffercopter - a big fish airstrike helicopter thing
!! do not try to kill it it's impossible !! 

Cow table - a hostile mob
Nothing remarkable about it other than the fact that it sometimes drops a microwave when killed

7. Crafting table 2: you can find it in a recipe book and can craft it after obtaining a crafting table and a furnace, its usage is pretty straight forward

On the right is the recipe book where you can find recipes for things (make sure to study it closely as it has quite a bit of important stuff!!!)

</details>
